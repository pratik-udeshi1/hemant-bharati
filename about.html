About Dr.Hemant Bharati

DR. Hemant Bharati graduated from government medical college Nagpur and obtained medical doctorate from G.S medical college of Mumbai. He has more than 25 Years of experience as chest physician. He is Ex-Director medical of ESIS in government health service. Dr. Hemant was chairman of occupational lung disease and medical board, Presently a life member of chest society of India. Dr. Hemant Bharati in a capacity of chairman organized several national and international conferences. He has extensively travelled and visited many medical institution abroad. Dr Hemant Bharati is expert in diagnosis and treatment of Asthma, Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, Interstitial lung disease, occupational lung disease, malignant and autoimmune lung disorders including tuberculosis, and infectious lung diseases. Dr. Hemant Bharati has special expertise in bronchoscopy and Sleep apnea.
